Hildah's Poultry Farm is a web-based management system designed to help poultry farmers digitize and streamline their daily farm operations. The platform enables farmers to track bird batches, monitor egg production, manage customer orders, and maintain sales records—all in one place.

🚀 Features
Batch Management: Track different groups of birds by age, breed, and health status

Production Tracking: Record daily egg collection and monitor trends

Inventory Control: View real-time stock of available eggs and birds

Customer Orders: Manage customer requests and process sales

Sales Records: Keep history of transactions and payments

Reports & Analytics: Generate insights on production and revenue

🖥️ How It Works
For Customers:
Visit the website to view available poultry products

Check current prices and stock availability
